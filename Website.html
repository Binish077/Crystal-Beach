Why be Sustainable?

Beaches are home to several different species of plants and animals. Furthermore, beaches provide protection to those living near the beach from strong winds due to storms. They are a hub of economic acitivty and are entire coastal ecosystems.

Fun Facts about Beaches

-Sand on beaches are mostly made of silica.

-River Thames in London used to have an artificial beach from the 1930's to the 70's until it was closed due to safety concerns.

-It was in the 18th century that beaches became popular tourist attractions
